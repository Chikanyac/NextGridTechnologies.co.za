5 Microsoft 365 Security Mistakes Small Businesses Make

1. Not Enforcing Multi-Factor Authentication
Without MFA, accounts are highly vulnerable to credential theft.

2. Too Many Global Administrators
Excess admin privileges increase risk exposure.

3. Default Spam Policies
Default protection is often not enough against modern phishing attacks.

4. No Conditional Access Policies
Users can log in from anywhere without restrictions.

5. Oversharing in OneDrive & SharePoint
External sharing can expose sensitive company data.
