Backup Strategies Every Business Should Use

Data loss can be catastrophic for any business. Learn the most effective backup strategies to protect your files and systems.

  • Cloud backups for automated offsite storage
  • External drives for local copies
  • Versioning to recover previous file versions
  • Regular backup schedules and testing

Implementing multiple layers of backup ensures your business can recover quickly from hardware failure, accidental deletion, or cyberattacks.
