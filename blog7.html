Phishing 101: How to Spot Scam Emails

Phishing emails are designed to trick users into giving sensitive information. Learn how to identify and avoid these scams.

  • Check the sender’s email address carefully
  • Look for spelling mistakes or unusual formatting
  • Never click links or download attachments from unknown sources
  • Verify suspicious requests via phone or official channels
  • Use email filters and security software

Training your team to recognize phishing attempts is one of the most effective defenses against cyberattacks.
